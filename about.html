Our Process

We have diversified in Epoxy and PU Flooring in 2015 with world class flooring solutions which has given us upper hand in providing surface enhancement of Flooring of Indian Industries.

Why choose ARYA TECH SOLUTIONS for your bussiness

To compete internationally we need to improve quality of final product and hence enhancement of surface is very important. Looking at this factor we have developed methods of pickling and passivation and mechanical polishing and Electropolishing of your fabricated stainless steel. Not only this to serve our customers batter we provide complete job work at your site and also offer you the chemicals for these processes.

To add to the above we haven an inhouse facility for Electropolishing of SS 304, 304L, 316 & 316L Stainless Steel. We offer EP of any components, Inside and outside of Pipes & Tubes upto 6mm ID. The Electrolyte is developed as per ASTM 912 to give Ra upto 0.20.
